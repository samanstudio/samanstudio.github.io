Saman Aboutalebi is an animation artist, music composer, art lecturer, graphic designer, and Producer. Since 2001, his work has been showcased at international festivals, and since 2003, he has collaborated with animation and game studios. He became a permanent member of ASIFA in 2011 and is currently teaching 2D and 3D techniques at the School of Art Berlin. He also organizes workshops and art events with live music and is passionate about art education and nurturing the next generation of creative talent.
