My name is Saman Aboutalebi. I'm a music composer, animation artist, director, graphic designer, and Illustrator.
Since 2001, my work has been showcased in international festivals and magazines. I’ve worked in animation and game studios since 2003, specializing in character design, 2D animation, directing, and music composition. I have been a proud International Animated Film Association (ASIFA) member since 2011.
